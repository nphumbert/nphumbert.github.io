Outline

1. What Makes a High-Performing Team?

Discover what sets high-performing teams apart from ordinary groups. You’ll get an effective framework to assess your team’s dynamics and establish the foundation for long-term success.

2. Positioning Your Team Within the Organization

Position your team to maximize its impact. You’ll learn to align daily operations with company strategy, define KPIs that drive real value, and steer clear of the common performance traps that hold teams back.

3. Designing How Your Team Works

Identify the team structures that best support your goals. You’ll understand how size, roles, and communication patterns influence performance, gain the know-how to design teams that remain nimble and resilient, and build the confidence to navigate each stage of the team lifecycle.

4. Leading and Managing for Lasting Impact

Explore the leadership styles that drive team success and know when to use each effectively. You’ll walk away with practical tools to foster intrinsic motivation, establish psychological safety, and create a culture of feedback, learning, and recognition that fuels engagement, innovation, and lasting success.

FAQ

Who is this course for?

This course is designed for executives and engineering leaders who want to optimize team performance and collaboration. It’s ideal for those looking to transform individual talent into a cohesive team aligned with the company’s strategic vision.

What if I have no prior experience?

No prior experience required. This course takes you step by step, making it fully accessible even if you’re starting from zero. All you need is the willingness to learn and put your new skills into practice.
