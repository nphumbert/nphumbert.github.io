Outline

1. What Makes a High-Performing Team?

Before you can build a high-performing team, you need to know what one looks like. This chapter explores the key characteristics that distinguish effective teams from ordinary work groups. You’ll gain a clear framework to assess your current team dynamics and lay the foundation for strategic improvement in the next chapters.

2. Positioning Your Team Within the Organization

Learn how to strategically position your team to maximize its impact. This chapter introduces tools to align daily operations with the company’s broader objectives. You’ll also discover how to define KPIs that actually drive value and avoid common performance traps.

3. Designing How Your Team Works

This chapter breaks down different team topologies and how to choose the right structure for your goals. You’ll learn how team size, role distribution, and communication patterns influence performance, and how to design teams that are both nimble and resilient. You’ll also explore the team lifecycle and how to navigate each stage effectively.

4. Leading and Managing for Lasting Impact

Explore the leadership styles that make a real difference, and when to use each one. This chapter also dives into motivation: how to foster intrinsic drive, build psychological safety, and design a culture of continuous feedback, learning, and recognition. You’ll walk away with practical tools to grow engagement, innovation, and long-term team success.
