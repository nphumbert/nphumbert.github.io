SIPOC

Core Idea

Before analyzing a complex process in detail, it is useful to define its boundaries. The SIPOC model provides a high-level overview by identifying five essential elements:

  • Suppliers
  • Inputs
  • Process
  • Outputs
  • Customers

Through these perspectives, SIPOC establishes a common understanding of what the process includes and what it does not.

Application

For any process under review, identify:

  • Who provides the inputs?
  • What resources or information are required?
  • What are the major process steps?
  • What outputs are produced?
  • Who receives these outputs?

This shared understanding prevents teams from optimizing different perceptions of the same process.

Takeaway

A well-defined process boundary is usually the first step toward solving the right problem.
