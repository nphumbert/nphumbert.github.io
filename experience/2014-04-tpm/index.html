Technical Program Manager

Description

Orchestrated key initiatives for France’s nationwide Linky smart meter project, collaborating with 100+ cross-functional stakeholders to deploy millions of connected meters.

Achievements

  • • Accelerated the rollout by aligning engineering, leadership, and product teams around shared goals.

  • • Boosted efficiency and adaptability by embedding Agile practices and streamlining processes.

  • • Raised product quality and reliability through software best practices and new monitoring features.

  • • Advanced France’s digital energy infrastructure by enabling the country’s first large-scale, multi-region smart meter deployment.

Project Details

  • Client Type : Large enterprise
  • Industry : Energy
  • Location : France
  • Started on : April 2014
  • Duration : 1 year 3 months
