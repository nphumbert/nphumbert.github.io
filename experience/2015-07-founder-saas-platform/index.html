Founder — SaaS Platform

Description

Developed a cloud-based invoice management platform that helped Honduran entrepreneurs stay compliant with new regulations and keep their businesses open.

Achievements

  • • Simplified legal compliance by automating complex requirements such as invoice numeration, formatting rules, and mandatory fields.

  • • Protected small businesses from fines and closures, enabling them to operate sustainably despite regulatory changes.

Project Details

  • Client : SMEs
  • Location : Honduras
  • Started on : July 2015
  • Duration : 1 year
