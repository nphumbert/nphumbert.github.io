Project Details

Description

After a few years in the tech industry, I started noticing some recurring needs not properly covered in the market. So, I decided to become self-employed and explore my hypothesis. After a year of exploration, I confirmed the potential of my offer and decided to create an entity to continue developing my business.

During my time running Crafties, I had the chance to develop several entrepreneurial skills such as:

  • • Defining a relevant business strategy

  • • Developing a client portfolio

  • • Managing and negotiating contracts

  • • Working closely with company executives and stakeholders at all organisational levels

  • • Establishing partnerships with other players in the market

  • • Gaining some insights into the accounting and legal aspects of the business
