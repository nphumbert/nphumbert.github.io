Overview

This topic investigates how organizations define and enable long-term direction under uncertainty. It integrates classical perspectives with contemporary approaches emphasizing agility, feedback, and continuous realignment. The analysis focuses on the translation of strategic intent into measurable objectives and governance mechanisms capable of sustaining consistency across multiple time horizons. Through comparative case evidence, the topic delineates how strategy can function simultaneously as an analytical process, a learning system, and a coordination device linking vision, structure, and performance outcomes.
