Overview

This topic addresses the integration of emerging technologies into organizational strategy and governance. It examines how digital infrastructures, data systems, and algorithmic decision-making reshape managerial processes and organizational boundaries. The analysis incorporates field experience and frameworks from information systems research, data ethics, and innovation management to assess readiness, risk, and value creation. Case studies of digital transformation in knowledge-intensive sectors illustrate the interdependence between technological capability and organizational learning. The objective is to articulate a framework for responsible technology deployment that aligns with strategic intent, regulatory requirements, and societal expectations.
