Business Strategy

I developed and executed the business strategy for my ventures, which include my tech consulting company, Crafties, and my environmental literacy platform, Time for a Change. Additionally, I advised other startups and SMEs, enabling their success and expansion.

Change Management

I effectively orchestrated strategic, organisational, and technological changes during the scale-up phases of startups, enabling them to leverage technology to seize new business opportunities.

Leadership

I spearheaded initiatives and led teams for the French national electricity project Linky (over 100 people), enabled continuous value delivery in a distributed team environment for a tech company, and coordinated a school renovation project in Honduras, among other endeavours.

Operational Excellence

As a consultant, I frequently adopted a systemic approach to analyse organisational dynamics from various perspectives, gaining deep insights into their needs. By effectively managing system constraints, I enhanced organisational processes to drive significant improvements and efficiencies.

Marketing
