About me.

Hi, I’m Nadia.

At 17, I moved to France — and I’ve been exploring the world for knowledge, ideas, and fresh perspectives ever since.

Over the past 15 years, I’ve lived, studied, and worked across four countries, earned an MBA and two Master of Science degrees, become proficient in five languages, and built deep expertise at the intersection of business, strategy, and technology.

Along the way, I founded two tech companies and a social enterprise, and supported 20+ high-growth businesses in securing funding, expanding internationally, and overcoming systemic strategic and operational challenges.

Today, I transform complexity and long-standing roadblocks into sustainable growth for tech-driven organizations — both as an executive advisor and through self-paced business training programs designed to help tech leaders scale, pivot, and lead change with efficiency and lasting impact.
